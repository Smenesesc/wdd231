Tank Surf Site Plan

Site Name

Tank Surf

Reason for Name: "Tank Surf" symbolizes strength , giving emphasis to a powerful surfing experience that's in tune with the water. The used of "Tank" represents the strenght and resistance you build in the sport of surfing.

Optional domain availability: tanksurf.com

Site Purpose

The website provides a online site for surf enthusiasts, showing the latest surfboard models, surf spots, training tips, and a community forum. The site will be for tutorials, gear reviews, and surfing-related news.

Scenarios

  • What are the best surfboards for beginners?
  • Where can I find the top surf spots near me?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #00796b (Teal) – used for headings, buttons, and links.

Secondary Color: #e0f7fa (Light Cyan) – used for backgrounds, card sections, and footer.

Typography

Font 1: Arial (for general text and body content)

Font 2: 'Courier New' (for wireframes and technical text)

Wireframe

Desktop View:

Header: Logo, Navigation Menu (Home, About, Contact)

Main Section: Hero Image, Featured Surfboards

Footer: Contact Info, Social Media Links

Mobile View:

Header: Logo, Hamburger Menu

Main Section: Scrollable Featured Surfboards

Footer: Compact Contact Info, Social Media Icons
